Beef Birria Tacos

Description

I love the vibrant flavors of Mexican cuisine. One of my favorites has to be Birria tacos. They're flavorful and juicy and just outright delicious!

If you're looking for something that gives you a nice crispy bite into its succulent insides, birria tacos are the way to go. Are you ready to give them a try?

Ingredients

Meat

  • 4 lbs. beef chuck roast
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Sauce

  • 6 dried guajillo chile peppers, seeded
  • 4 dried chile de arbol peppers, stemmed and seeded
  • 2 dried ancho chiles, stemmed and seeded
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 medium Roma tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 whole cloves
  • pinch of ground cinnamon
  • pinch of ground cumin
  • pinch of ground thyme
  • pinch of dried marjoram
  • pinch of dried oregano
  • pinch of salt

Tacos

  • 18 corn tortillas
  • 1 large white onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded queso asadero (white Mexican cheese)
